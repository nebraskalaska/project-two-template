Admission

Exhibitions are free and open to the public. Performances and events may require admission or advance ticket sales.

Getting Here

We are located in the Kenton neighborhood of North Portland, directly across from the Yellow Line MAX train at the Kenton/ N Denver Avenue stop.

Onsite and street parking is available. For events after 6pm you may park in the Wells Fargo lot next door. The gallery entrance is located on the southwest side of the building between Interstate Avenue and McClellan Street, within the green space.
